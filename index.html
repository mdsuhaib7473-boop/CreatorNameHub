Frequently Asked Questions

Is CreatorNameHub really free?

Yes! CreatorNameHub is 100% free forever. There are no hidden charges, premium tiers, or credit limits. We believe in supporting creators without barriers.

Do I need to sign up or create an account?

No sign-up is required. You can start generating names immediately. Your favorites and history are saved locally in your browser.

How accurate is the username availability?

Our tool generates creative variations, but it does not check real-time availability on social platforms. Always verify directly on the platform before committing to a username.

Can I use the generated names for my business?

Yes, you are free to use any names generated. However, we strongly recommend conducting a trademark search and checking domain availability to ensure the name is legally available for your use.

How do I save my favorite names?

Click the "Favorite" (heart) icon next to any generated name. It will be saved to your browser's local storage and can be viewed on the Favorites page.

Who is behind CreatorNameHub?

CreatorNameHub was founded by Suhaib, a brand strategist based in India with 8+ years of experience in brand development. You can learn more on our About Us page or contact him directly at hello@creatornamehub.com.

How are the names generated?

Our generators use a combination of curated word databases, naming pattern algorithms, and style-based filters to create relevant and creative suggestions based on your input. All generation happens directly in your browser no data is sent to our servers. The suggestions are designed to be diverse, covering different naming styles (modern, creative, professional, playful, and more).

Should I do a trademark search before using a name?

Yes absolutely, especially for business names. Our tool provides creative inspiration, but it cannot check trademark databases. Before committing to a business name, search your country's trademark registry (e.g., USPTO in the USA, IPO in the UK, IP India for India). Read our Trademark Beginner's Guide in the blog for a step-by-step overview.

Can I export my generated names?

Yes! On any results page, you'll find "Export JSON" and "Export CSV" buttons that download all generated names as a file. This is useful for sharing with collaborators or keeping a record of your brainstorming sessions.

Does CreatorNameHub work on mobile?

Yes, the entire site is fully optimised for mobile devices. You can generate names, save favourites, read blog articles, and export results from any smartphone or tablet. The interface adapts automatically to your screen size.

My favorites disappeared. What happened?

Favorites and history are stored in your browser's local storage, not on our servers. This means they are tied to the specific browser and device you were using. If you cleared your browser data, switched browsers, or used a different device, your saved names will not be there. We recommend copying or exporting important names to a separate document for safekeeping.

How do I contact CreatorNameHub?

You can reach us at hello@creatornamehub.com or via the Contact page. We aim to respond within 24 hours on weekdays. We welcome feedback, bug reports, feature requests, and general questions.
