3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide our services: Your keyword inputs are used solely to generate name suggestions for you. We do not store or share these inputs with third parties.
  • To improve our tools: Anonymous usage data helps us understand which tools are most popular so we can improve them.

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for marketing purposes.

4. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies (small text files stored on your device) to:

  • Remember your theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Remember your saved names (stored locally on your device, not our servers)
  • Collect anonymous analytics about how visitors use our site (via Google Analytics)
  • Serve relevant ads via Google AdSense

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling cookies may affect some features of the website (such as saving your preferred theme).

We use Google Analytics to understand site traffic. Google Analytics collects data anonymously. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.
